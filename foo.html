RubyConf 2023

RubyConf 2023 ended this evening. I like conferences for exposing me to new topics, such that when I encounter them at work, they feel less intimidating. I also like them for networking. I still follow along the work of people I met at the Denver 2021 conference. This RubyConf* was in San Diego. I attended the livestream, which cost $1001 and provided access to the main stage and Slack community. Here’s a summary of selected presentations:

Keynote by Yukihiro “Matz” Matsumoto

Matz, the creator of Ruby, cares deeply about its continued popularity . He gave BaseCamp, Shopify, and GH as examples of successful companies built on Ruby. He says Ruby is optimized to start small.

May Ruby remain attractive for future companies. - Matz
