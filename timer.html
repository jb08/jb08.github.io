Parental leave and Sabattical Benefits

  1. Parental leaves are 4 months long for birthing parents (slightly less for non-birthing parents).
  2. Employees are eligible for sabattical after 3,6,9 years for durations of 1,2,3 months, respectively.
  3. Employees cannot take parental leave and sabbatical in the same rolling 12 month period.
  4. Sabatticals are to be used in the 12 months following their 3,6,9 year anniversary.
